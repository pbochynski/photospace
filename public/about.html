Why photo libraries get out of control

Duplicates everywhere

Messenger saves, burst shots, edits, exports – they pile up silently and cost storage.

Manual cleanup is painful

Scrolling, comparing, and second‑guessing every image is slow and error‑prone.

Privacy concerns

Most tools upload your photos to the cloud to analyze them. That's a hard no.

How Photospace fixes it

AI runs in your browser

CLIP‑based visual similarity and quality scoring execute locally with WebGPU/WASM. Your photos never leave your device.

  • Find true and near‑duplicates
  • Detect large photo series and bursts
  • Pick the best shot using sharpness/exposure/face quality

Built for OneDrive

Direct Microsoft Graph integration. Browse, scan, and clean without exports or sync friction.

  • Auto‑index while you browse
  • Queue recursive folder scans (parallel)
  • Service Worker caching for snappy previews

Fast, focused cleanup

Groups similar shots, highlights the best, and pre‑selects the rest for removal. Delete confidently with one click.

  • Similarity & time‑window controls
  • Date range filtering
  • Keyboard navigation in a full‑screen viewer

Private by design

Photospace does all analysis locally. No uploads, no servers, no training on your photos. Embeddings and metadata live in your browser (IndexedDB), and you can export/import them to speed up work across devices.
